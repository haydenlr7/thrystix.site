Joey Gardener - Guitar and Vocals

Joey’s skills on the six string are unmatched, he is full of riffs and ideas that turn into the band’s next hit. Growing up on classic metal has driven him to keep the genre alive. Joey was also one of the founding members of Thrystix.

Winnie Liza - Bass and Backing Vocals

Wennie is a multi-talented musician, with years of experience and a music degree under her belt, she brings together the musical foundation of Thrystix. Thrystix couldn’t exist without a strong backbone like Wennie.

Hayden Robinson - Drums and Backing Vocals

Hayden has been surrounded by drumming his whole life. His father has been in many metal bands in his younger years, and his skills live on through Hayden. Hayden’s groove and power behind the kit is unmatched. Hayden is the other founding member of Thrystix.

Dani Cabaub - Photographer

Dani may not play an instrument, but she is a master with her tool. A long time friend of Hayden, Dani has no fear jumping into the crowd of “moshers” just to get the perfect shot of the band. All photos of the band are taken by her.
