Thrystix is a Heavy Metal band originating from Columbia SC, formed in 2024 by guitarist Joey Gardener, and drummer Hayden Robinson. They were joined by bassist Wennie just prior to the summer of ‘24, and have been thrashing together ever since. Their biggest inspirations are bands from the legendary Metallica, Anthrax, and Pantera, all the way to the groovy hair metal of Motley Crue and W.A.S.P.

Here the band can be seen practicing together for an upcoming gig. Just like the good ol’ days of classic metal bands in the 80’s, they’re practicing in Hayden’s garage. A true garage-band experience. Photo Credit: Dani Cabaub
